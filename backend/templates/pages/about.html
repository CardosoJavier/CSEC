About the
Cybersecurity Club

The Cybersecurity Club is your gateway to a successful and fulfilling career in the field of cybersecurity! We are here to provide you with the tools, resources, and support you need to succeed. Through a variety of academic, professional, and social events, you will have the opportunity to develop your skills, build your network, and have fun along the way.

Vision

Having CSEC be THE place to go to for an inclusive, diverse community of bright cybersecurity talent.

Mission

The purpose of CSEC is to develop and maintain and inclusive, diverse community promoting cybersecurity education, industry connections, and early career development through programs and activities.

Is it possible to participate without
prior knowledge of cybersecurity?


Regardless of their background or level of expertise. With a
focus on teaching, mentoring, and providing resources, the
club helps its members grow and succeed in the field.


No prior knowledge of computers or engineering is required, as
many of our members have started with little to no knowledge
of cybersecurity and have gone on to become club leaders and
secure jobs in the industry.
